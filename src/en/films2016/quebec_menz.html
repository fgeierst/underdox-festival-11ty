Eduardo Menz


Eduardo Menz was born in 1977 in Edmonton, Alberta from Chilean descent and has called Montréal home for the past nine years. In 2007, he co-founded Ctrllab, an exhibition space for emerging Montréal artists. He is a member of the Double Negative Collective. Eduardo’s film work has always attempted to transgress and interweave the boundaries of what defines fiction, documentary and experimental film genres.
