Lass den Sommer nie wieder kommen  

Let the Summer Never Come Again

 


Alexandre Koberidze

DE/GE 2017 – Mobile device  – 202 min – Georgian

Grand Prix du Jury, Prix
Premier, FID Marseille 2017

B+DOP+E: Alexandre Koberidze
S: Giorgi Koberidze – P: Alexandre Koberidze (dffb) – D: Josephine Settemcher (dffb) – With Mate
Kevlishvili, Giorgi Bochorishvili

           


 


As a matter of fact, the film never abandons its impressive narrative
simplicity, clearly stating a preference for the use of such stylization
in order to let something else spring forth: cinema itself. Shot in low
resolution, so that the colors form silent, unlimited force fields enabling
all drama to provide its own illustration through looks, gestures and
cinema's own means, this first film is quite striking in its propensity
to create uninterrupted wonders and charms, never ceasing to let
elementary fiction be nourished with documentary realities gleaned
while shooting. (Jean-Pierre Rehm)

wednesday 11 oct 8.30 p.m. werkstattkino
