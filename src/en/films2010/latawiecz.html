LATAWIEC

Maria Frycz | D 2007 | 5 min.

Material: Super-8
Format: 35mm
Original language: no dialogues

Camera: Maria Frycz
Editing: Maria Frycz
Sound: Maria Frycz, Benjamin Greber, Jan Linneman

Production: Maria Frycz
Print/Sales: Maria Frycz

www.mariafrycz.com

 

‘Latawiec’ is an experimental short film, telling a simplified story of person, which is spending a windy, grey day at the seaside – alone in the landscape. He is drinking hot tea, watching his hands and flying a kite. There is a strange tension between the character and the object – what at first seems to be a game, turns out to be a fight, which takes place somewhere between reality and dream.

 

Maria Frycz , born in 1981in Zielona Góra, Poland. Studies of Fine Art in Avignon, Lyon and Münster (Fylmklasse). She lives in Berlin.

Films: 2009 Spaces, Passage | 2007 Latawiec | 2006 Peut-être, Not Titled Yet | 2005 Schnee | 2004 Hairdresser
