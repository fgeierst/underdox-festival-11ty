Kreis Wr.Neustadt

A to A

Johann Lurf – AT 2011 – 5 min.

C: Mark Gerstorfer – E+P: Johann Lurf – S: Nils Kirchhoff – D: Sixpackfilm

35mm/3D on DCP – no dialogues

www.johannlurf.net

 

 

 

Hilarious overview of architectural failures. A road movie that provides new insights, but goes nowhere. Johann Lurf once again reveals his striking talent and his sense of humor. "A to A" is an extensive catalogue of mediocre architectural objects on roundabouts, competing for attention in the few seconds as they are passed. A unique artist's impression, shot from a spluttering Vespa. – International Film Festival Rotterdam 2012

 

Johann Lurf, born in 1982. Since 2002 he has studied at Vienna's Academy of Fine Arts. 2009 diploma at Harun Farocki's filmclass.

Films (selection): Vertigo Rush 2007 – 12 Explosionen 2008 (4. UNDERDOX) – Zwölf Boxkämpfer jagen… 2009 (5. UNDERDOX) – Endeavour 2010 – Kreis Wr.Neustadt 2011
