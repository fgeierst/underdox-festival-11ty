Sirmilik


Zacharias Kunuk – CA 2010 – 10 min.

Sc: Zacharias Kunuk – C: Steve Cosens, Russell Gienapp – E: Andres Landau – M: Andrew Whiteman, Dean Stone, Tanya Tagaq

HDCam – Inuktitut

 

 

 

 

 

Shot and soundtracked in Nunavut’s Sirmilik National Park, this short film explores the changes in the Arctic’s icy expanses, skies and Inuit communities, and the beauty of the Canadian north. "Sirmilik" was produced as one of 13 films that together make up the National Parks Project. – Forum Berlinale

 

Zacharias Kunuk, born 1957 in Kapuivik, Canada. He lives and works in Igloolik. His film Atanarjuat: "The Fast Runner" (2001), the first feature film ever to be written and shot entirely in Inuktitut won several international awards.
