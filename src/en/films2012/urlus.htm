Deep REd


Esther Urlus – NL 2012 – 7 min.

K+P: Esther Urlus

35mm

German Premiere

 

 

 

 

"Deep Red" is an investigation into additive colour mixing (a kind of silkscreen technique) but also a successful poetic work that evokes experiences from childhood. – IFF Rotterdam

 

Esther Urlus, born in 1966 in the Netherlands, makes 16mm films and installations. Esther is co-founder and leader of the WORM.filmstudio, a Rotterdam-based workshop for experimental film and the DIY method, and one of the few places left in Europe where you can work with Super-8 and 16mm film.

Films (selection): Abandoned Interiors 2001 –Idyll 2008 – Deep Red 2012
