Naomi Steuer


The Invisible Hinge – 2009 – 16 min.

C: Balarama Heller, Naomi Steuer – E: Naomi Steuer

 

 

 

 

 

 

At the age of nine, Naomi Steuer accompanied her mother on a three months’ travel through India. The trip got to be the uniting element between mother’s and daughter’s relationship. Twenty years later, the artist set off to make this trip a second time with photographer Blarama Heller.

 

Naomi Steuer, born in 1979 in Munich. Study of Media Arts at the AdK, Munich, of film in Istanbul and of ducomentary at the HfG Karlsruhe. She lives and works in Munich.

Balarama Heller, born in 1979 in New York. Study of photography in Boston. He travels as documentary photographer through Eastern Europe, Turkey and India. He lives and works in New York.
