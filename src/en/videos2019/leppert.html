Laura Leppert


Laura Leppert   born in 1993 in Henstedt-Ulzburg, Schleswig-Holstein. She lives and works in Munich. Studies at the University of Art Linz, at the Muthesius Academy of Fine Arts Kiel and at the Academy of Fine Arts Munich (class Olaf Nicolai).

Empty Hand

2018 | Video, Sound
5 min | English 
Text: Laura Leppert | Image: Laura Leppert, Found Footage

Gods are characterized by their attributes; film actions are driven by the hunt for objects. But what is central when the object of desire is interchangeable? Empty hands show, present, grab, grab and pull, tear open packaging and surrender secrets. (Laura Leppert)
