work in progress

 

It is about burning matresses and petrified electro appliances, about animated travelling bags and bursted bathroom mirrors, about turbines and radiators, about raincoats and straitjackets, about sound checks and performance experiments, about expeditions and projections and here and there about the right colour application: 22 female artists are talking about their art.

 

Ulrich Mannes, born in 1965 in Memmingen, lives since 1985 in Munich,
editor of the magazine SigiGötz-Entertainment. Film maker since 1997 (shortfilms).


Wolfgang Leitner, born in 1960 in Augsburg. Study of American Literature and Sociology. Camera operator at various short films.

Films by Mannes (selection): Im Schatten des Plüschtiers 1997 Sigi Götz Collectors Item 1998 Die Übergabe 2001 Çatlak 2003 Das Mädchen mit der elektromagnetischen Ausstrahlung - Ein Trailer 2006

Films by Leitner: Festtag 1999 Rockstar 2004
