artist in focus: GUILLAUME CAILLEAU
wednesday 17okt 8:30 p.m werkstattkino

Guillaume Cailleau
On the trail of the American avant-garde, the French-Berlin experimental filmmaker, master student of Heinz Emigholz, member of Labor Berlin and producer of Ben Russell, finds his way to poetic-material impressions of everyday life, science and memories.
