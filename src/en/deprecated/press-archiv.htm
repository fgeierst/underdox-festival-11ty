Accreditation

All reporting film journalists receive free press accreditation. We ask trade visitors and other press for a small donation or a contribution in any another form (film tips, bring your friends, word of mouth, likes, sharing etc.) to support our precious festival work.

Accreditations for the press and trade visitors can be applied informally by e-mail, stating name, medium, address and phone number.
Applications to press [at] underdox-festival.de
