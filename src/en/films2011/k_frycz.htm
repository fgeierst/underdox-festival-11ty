berlin-Tempelhof


Maria Frycz | D 2011 | 13 min.

Material: Super16
Format: Super16
Original language: no dialogues


Camera: Maria Frycz
Editing: Maria Frycz
Sound: Ralf Willner

Production: Maria Frycz

Print/Sales: Maria Frycz

German Premiere

 

 

 

"Berlin-Tempelhof" is a documentary about a closed airport in central Berlin. It is a short film dealing with urban space, which has lost it's purpose and functions as a landscape of memory and time. – Maria Frycz

 

Maria Frycz , born in 1981in Zielona Góra, Poland. Studies of Fine Art in Avignon, Lyon and Münster (Fylmklasse). She lives in Berlin.

Films (selection): Hairdresser 2004 | Schnee 2005 | Peut-être, Not Titled Yet 2006 | Latawiec (UNDERDOX 05) 2007  | Spaces, Passage 2009  | Tempelhof 2011
