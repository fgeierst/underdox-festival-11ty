Führung (Guided tour)


René Frölke | D 2010 | 37 min.

Material: HDCam
Format: BluRay
Original language: German


Camera: René Frölke
Editing: René Frölke
Sound: Robert Nickolaus

With Horst Köhler, Peter Sloterdijk, Peter Weibel

Production: Ann Carolin Renninger, joon film
Print/Sales: joon film

Awards: Ferroni Brigade Golden Donkey Foal, Berlinale 2011

www.joonfilm.de

 

 

 

"In October 2008, at the height of an economic crisis, the stat's highest official visits an art school. A document of its times." (René Frölke)

 

René Frölke, born in 1978 in Eichsfeld, Germany. Since 1999 freelancer editor and cameraman for television and documentary film projects, including for Thomas Heise.

Films (selection): Jour de grève 2007 |  Ropinsalmi 2007 |  Von der Vermählung des Salamanders mit der grünen Schlange 2010 |  Führung 2010
