choses qui me rattachent aux Etres
(Things that Connect Me with Beings)


Boris Lehman | BE 2010 | 15 min.

Material: 16mm
Format: DigiBeta
Original language: French

Script: Boris Lehman
Camera: Antoine-Marie Meert
Editing: Ariane Mellet
Sound: Jacques Dapoz

With Boris Lehman

Production: DovFilm

Print/Sales: DovFilm

www.borislehman.be

 

 

An inventory of objects from the filmmaker's daily life that have been entrusted to him and which, over time, have become a collection.

I want to create an object with an image or word, by filming it. I show the camera some objects from my everyday life – things that once belonged to other people I loved or had a lot to do with – and I say, "I am the sum of everything the others have given me." What mysterious relationship is there between these objects? Between these objects and me?” – Boris Lehman

 

Boris Lehman, born in 1944 in Lausanne. He studied at the INSAS film academy in Brussels. Boris Lehman creates, produces and distributes his films as an independent filmmaker. To date, his work – filmed mostly on Super 8 mm and 16 mm – comprises some 400 films: full-length features, short subjects, documentaries, essay films, experimental films, diaries and autobiographies.

Films (selection): La clé du champ 1963 | Symphonie 1979 | Couple, Regards, Positions 1983 | A la recherche du lieu de ma naissance 1990 | Leçon de vie 1995 | A comme Adrienne 1999 | Homme portant 2003 | Tentatives de se décrire 2005 | Survie 2007
