mouse palace


Harald Hund, Paul Horn | AT 2010 | 11 min.

Material: DV
Format: DigiBeta
Original language: no dialogues

Script: Harald Hund, Paul Horn
Camera: Harald Hund, Paul Horn
Editing:Harald Hund, Paul Horn
Sound Design: Harald Hund, Paul Horn

Production: Harald Hund

Print/Sales: Sixpack

www.haraldhund.com/weblog

 

 

Bizarre relationship drama featuring mice in a purpose-built apartment with edible furnishings. From the "Living Space series" by Hund and Horn.

 

Harald Hund, born in 1967 in Austria. He studied art and new media in Vienna. He makes music videos and short animation films.

Films (selection): Gorilla will fressen 1999 | Urlaub 2000 | Dackel Du 2001 Villalog 2002 | All People Is Plastic 2005

Paul Horn, born in 1966. He studied painting and scenography in Vienna and works as a video artist. Since 2002, he has been collaborating often with Harald Hund.

Common films: Tomatenköpfe 2002 | Habibi Kebab 2004 | Dropping Furniture | Mouse Palace 2010
