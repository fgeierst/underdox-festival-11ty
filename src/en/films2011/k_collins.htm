Marxism today (Prologue)


Phil Collins | D 2010 | 35 min.

Material: DV
Format: DigiBeta
Original language: German

Script: Phil Collins
Camera: Matthias Schellenberg
Editing: Cristóvão A. dos Reis
Sound: Jochen Jezussek

With Petra Mgoza-Zeckay, Andrea Ferber, Marianne Klotz

Production: Phil Collins
Print/Sales: Shady Lane

 

 

The world changed when the wall came down. In "Marxism Today", a number of Marxism-Leninism teachers and a sportswoman from the DDR who saw their professional lives change radically or even end after 'Die Wende' take a disconcerted look back. Virtuoso interpolation of exceptional archival material.

 

Phil Collins, born in 1970 in the United Kingdom, works as a director, photographer, interviewer and producer and currently lives in Berlin. In 2006 he was nominated for the Turner Prize.

Films (selection): Return of the Real 2005 | He Who Laughs Last Laughs Longest 2006 | Soy mi madre 2009 | Marxism Today 2010
