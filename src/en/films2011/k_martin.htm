Tu as loué une voiture pour pleurer
(Crying in our cars)


Isabelle Martin | BE 2010 | 15 min.

Material: 16mm
Format: DigiBeta
Original language: French

Script: Olivier Moulin, Isabelle Martin
Camera: Marie Celette, Isabelle Martin
Editing: Olivier Moulin, Isabelle Martin
Sound: Brice Cannavo

Text: Isabelle Martin

Production: Isabelle Martin

Print/Sales: Isabelle Martin

German Premiere

 

 

A film about the place where to cry.
Where do we cry ?

Sometimes you cry in other people’s homes.
You phone up and you say:
I don’t want them to hear me, I don’t want them to see me,
could you give me the keys to your place?

In return you clean up, you vacuum a little,
or you leave a few chocolates on the kitchen table.

You have several sets of keys, several crying places,
bags full of gifts to cry in others’ homes.

 

Isabelle Martin, born in 1978 in Brussels .  After studying "visual narration" at the Ecole de Recherche Graphique in Brussels (1996 - 2000), she enters the Insas in film directing (2002 - 2006). Writing is at the core of her work. A writing which takes different forms, and expresses itself through several artistic disciplines: film and video, performance and sound work focusing on the voice, text.

Films (selection): Je danse devant toi toujours 1998 | La maison de carton 2000 |  Ne plus aimer la neige 2004 |  Toute grande toute seule 2005 |  Tu as loué une voiture pour pleurer 2010
