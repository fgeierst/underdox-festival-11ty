Oral History


Volko Kamensky | D 2009 | 22 min.

Material: 35mm
Format: 35mm
Original language: German

Script: Volko Kamensky
Camera: Uli Fischer
Editing: Volko Kamensky
Sound: Julian Rohrhuber

Production: Volko Kamensky

Print/Sales: Volko Kamensky

Awards: Best Short Film, Regensburg 2011

 

 

Is there such a thing as a collective memory? And, assuming there is, would it be possible to represent it in some way? To question it? For Volko Kamensky, the answer is self-evident: Of course!
A report from the land of the Brothers Grimm.

 

Volko Kamensky, born in 1972. Studied at Academy of Fine Arts in Hamburg (Germany). Worked as filmmaker, visual artist and production designer.

Films (selection): Divina Obsesión 1999 | Alles was wir haben 2004 | Oral History 2009
