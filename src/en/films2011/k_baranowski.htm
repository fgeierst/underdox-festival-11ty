Rauschgift (Addicted)


Peter Baranowski | D 2011 | 22 min.

Material: MiniDV
Format: DigiBeta
Original language: German, Italian, French

Script: Peter Baranowski
Camera: Aline Lászlo
Editing: Peter Baranowski
Sound: Peter Baranowski

With Marielle Timm

Production: Passanten Filmproduktion, HFF München
Print/Sales: Passanten Filmproduktion

Awards: Pardino d’oro, Locarno 2011, Prize of Film Criticism Guild of Russia, Kansk 2011, Special Mention, Starter Filmpreis 2011

 

 

 

Told in a reduced narrative style, this trip starts in rainy Germany and eventually takes us to the dodgy roads in the Moroccan Rif-Mountains. Following a path usually used for drug-trafficking, a man tries to reach out for his fading love.

 

Peter Baranowski, born in 1979 in Frankfurt/Main, Germany. He initially studied physics and Arabic language and literature at the Universities of Heidelberg, Berlin and Oxford. Since 2006 he he studies film and TV direction at the University of Television and Film (HFF) in Munich. In 2009 he co-founded Passanten Filmproduktion together with Michael Krummenacher.

Films (selection): West-Östlicher Diwan 2007 | Öl auf Leinwand 2009 | Neulich in meiner Wohnung 2010| Rauschgift 2011
