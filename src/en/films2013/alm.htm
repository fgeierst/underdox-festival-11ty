Alm Schweben



 


Kai Miedendorp – D 2013 – 7 Min. – o. Dialoge

K+S+T: Kai Miedendorp

 

 

 

 

 

The humanisation of the Alm (alpine pasture) is the theme for a critical reflection on our wanderlust. In the rhythmic reanimation, imagery oscillate continually, acquire its meaning and lose it again, reshape and change its connotations. What emerges in the course of processing, completion, and assimilation is something like a particle of our own past, which explores the complexity of this process and the kind of awareness we float through when we retrace the metamorphoses of our visual memory regarding our relationship to nature. – Kai Miedendorp

 

Kai Miedendorp, born in 1980 in München. Studies of journalism, cinematographer, documentary and video-art in Munich, Berlin, Prague and Los Angeles.

Films (selection): The Aldeburgh Dragon 2010 (6. UX) – En Gamle Rød Huset 2011 – Pfad – Vorgarten 2012 – Alm Schweben 2013
