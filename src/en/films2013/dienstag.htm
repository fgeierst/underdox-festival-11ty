Dienstag und ein bisschen Mittwoch

Tuesday and a little bit of wednesday

 

 

Susanne Quester – D/Korea 2007 – 40 Min. – korean. OmU



D+S+E: Susanne Quester – C: Mieko Azuma – S: Yun-Hui Choi – P: Mandarinenfilm with HFF München

16mm – Korean

www.mandarinenfilm.de

 

 

TUESDAY shows a day in school life of young Suzie in South Korea who is preparing the contest for the entrance to the Academy of Arts. In between the hard working and painting classes at high school she dreams her away with poetic mangas, telling of a better life.

 

Susanne Quester, born in 1979 in Starnberg. She completed film studies at HFF Munich and works as director, author and curator.

Films (selection): Finow 2002 – DIENSTAG und ein bisschen mittwoch 2007 – Hotel Arnold 2008 – Eiki – vielleicht nach Japan 2010 – Heidis Land 2012 (8. UX)
