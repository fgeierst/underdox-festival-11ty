Okin geht zum Friseur

Okin goes to the hairdresser



 

Jakob Schreier – D 2012 — 10 Min. – dt. OF — 16mm/DVD

R: Jakob Schreier – K: Lorenz Weissfuss Mit Okin Cznupolowsky

www.serbischerstudent.de

 

 

 

 

 

Portrait of musician and artist Okin Cznupolowsky, post-avant-garde pop singer and „the world’s best solo musician“  (self-advertisement). The film plays within the small gap between tragedy and comic, irony and reality, reflection and naivety, and genius and nonsense.

 

Graphic: Okin Cznupolowsky

 

Jakob Schreier, studies at the HFF München.
