Heidis land – eine Reise

In Heidi's Country – A Journey

 

 

Susanne Quester – Deutschland 2012 –  48 Min. – dt. OF

R+B+S: Susanne Mi-Son Quester – K: Thomas Plenert – T: Magnus Pflüger, Claudia Leder – P: if… Productions mit HFF München
Super16mm / HD – Deutsch

www.mandarinenfilm.de

 

„Mountains don’t have names“, says goat-herd Peter to Heidi when she sees for the first time the amazing panorama of the Swiss alps and asks him for the names. A journey back to the time of lecture and summer holidays, and to the country of „Heidi“. We now realise: only in the moment when we are grown up, we really understand that we were lucky as a child.

 

 

Susanne Quester, born in 1979 in Starnberg. She completed film studies at HFF Munich and works as director, author and curator.

Films (selection): Finow 2002 – DIENSTAG und ein bisschen mittwoch 2007 – Hotel Arnold 2008 – Eiki – vielleicht nach Japan 2010 – Heidis Land 2012 (8. UX)
