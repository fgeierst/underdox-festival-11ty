50th Anniversary of Oberhausen Disaccord

 


Markus Mischkowski & Kai Maria Steinkühler – D 2012 – 2 Min. – BluRay

 

 

 

 

The outtakes from the famous Oberhausen press conference, February 28, 1962 - late hour remake in the Cologne headquarter of westendfilme.

 

Markus Mischkowski,  born in 1966 in Cologne, Germany. He is member of Filmclub 813 and the „Groupe Cologne“.

Kai Maria Steinkühler, born in 1966 in Cologne, Germany. He is member of Filmclub 813 and the „Groupe Cologne“.
