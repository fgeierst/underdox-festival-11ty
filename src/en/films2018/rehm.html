The bird and us

 


Félix Rehm

FR 2016

20 min – French

M: Géry Petit, Axel Nouveau– S: Félix Rehm – T: Romain Ozanne – P: La fémis


tuesday 16 oct 8.30 pm werkstattkino

Félix Rehm explores the history of Brancusi's sculpture, which was stopped by the American customs authorities in 1926. The sculpture should be declared as non-figurative and thus as finished goods. Therefore Brancusi went to court.


Félix Rehm   Born in France. Studies currently film directing at Fémis. First short films as director, camera operator and editor, amongst others with Clément Cogitore.
