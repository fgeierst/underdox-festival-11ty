From Tomorrow On, I Will  
Chun nuan hua kai



Wu Linfeng, Ivan Marković

DE, CN, RS 2019
First Steps Award 2019
60 min | HD | Mandarin

B: Ivan Marković, Tanja Šljivar, Wu Linfeng | DOP: Ivan Marković | E: Ivan Marković, Wu Linfeng, Gang Hengju
S: Zeng Chaopeng | P: Ivan Marković, Wu Linfeng, Fang Li | D: Ivan Marković | With Li Chuan

Tuesday | 15 oct 6.30 pm | Werkstattkino 


The film takes aim at a rapidly modernising China as a small-town transplant struggles with Beijing’s sprawling rhythms. Although Ivan Marković and Wu Linfeng’s story is also universal. The writing/directing duo’s first feature focuses on the minutiae of its city setting, while channelling the inertia and malaise of feeling out of place in an ever-changing world. (Screen Daily)


Wu Linfeng   born 1989 in Hunan, China, studied film. FROM TOMORROW ON, I WILL is his first feature-length film.


Ivan Marković   born 1989 in Belgrade. In 2012 he graduated from the University of the Arts in Belgrade with a degree in Camera Studies, followed by studies in Art and Media, Berlin. Ivan Marković works as a cameraman with Angela Schanelec and Dane Komljen. He works in Belgrade and Berlin.
