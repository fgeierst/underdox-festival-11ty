Saladdin Castique

 


Anssi Kasitonni

Finland 2018
6 min | HD | Finnish

B+DOP+E: Anssi Kasitonni | D: AV-arkki
With Hertta Heinonen, Pentti Heinonen

Tuesday 15 oct | 8.30 pm | Werkstattkino | Short Film Night


A man walks in the forest and tries to find berries. He finds instead an empty French salad dressing bottle. A genie comes out of the bottle and grants the man three wishes. The man uses the wishes traditionally but he uses bad language all the time and the genie does not like it. The man is going to learn a lesson.


Anssi Kasitonni   born 1978. The skateboarder and award-winning artist from Sahalahti, Finland, is a musician, draughtsman and sculptor. His fans love the films he makes on his own farm. They deal with ancient questions of morality, love and death. In 2011 he received the Ars Fennica Award.
