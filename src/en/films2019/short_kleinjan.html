Above Us Only Sky 

 


Arthur Kleinjan

NL, CZ 2019
28 min | HD | English

B+DOP+E+S+P+D: Arthur Kleinjan
M: Michael Galasso
www.arthurkleinjan.nl


Tuesday 15 oct | 6.30 pm | werkstattkino | With FROM TOMORROW ON, I WILL


A narrator leads the viewer into a magical-realist story that is bereft of fabrication. Above Us Only Sky questions the surreal logic of chance, repetition of events, and synchronicity. It blurs the boundaries that separate us from the past through a present that shifts and fluctuates between reality, imagination, and speculation.


Arthur Kleinjan   born 1970 in Rotterdam, is a visual artist. Studied at the art academies in Rotterdam and Amsterdam. His photographic and video works have been exhibited internationally, including solo exhibitions in Vienna, Vancouver and Berlin. ABOVE US ONLY SKY is the cinema version of his 3-channel video installation of the same name.
