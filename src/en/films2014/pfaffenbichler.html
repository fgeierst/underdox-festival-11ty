A Messenger From The Shadows (Notes on Film 06 A/Monologue 01)

 

Norbert Pfaffenbichler – Austria 2013 – 60 min.


E: Norbert Pfaffenbichler – M: Bernhard Lang – V: Sixpackfilm

DCP – no dialogue

German Premiere

 

 

 

The flow of the cut, congenially set to Bernd Lang’s soundtrack, thundering from refraction to film symphony, unites powerful images from master directors, such as Tod Browning, and camera geniuses, for instance, James Wong Howe, from the productions made distinct first and foremost by Chaney into a bizarre tragedy, whose surreal logic offers no escape. Emerging is an overwhelming (meta) nightmare, kaleidoscopically composing the handed-down remains of a magnificent inheritance into a ride on a ghost train through impossible spaces with paradoxical characters, both of which are newly created by, of all things, entirely classical montage techniques. A dance of death whose most diabolic punch line is inevitable resurrection – the definitive Phantom Ride. – Christoph Huber

Saturday 11/10 9.30 p.m. Filmmuseum

Norbert Pfaffenbichler, born 1967 in Steyr, Austria. 1994-2001 University of Applied Arts, MK for Media. Artist and curator, various participations in festivals and exhibitions, founding member of VIDOK and lanolin.

Films (selection): notes on film 01 else 2002 (6. UX) – Notes on Film 02 2006 (6. UX) – Mosaik mécanique 2007 (8. UX) – Conference Notes on film 05 2011 (7. UX) – Git Cut Noise 2011 – Intermezzo (Notes on Film 04) 2012 – A Messenger From The Shadows (Notes on Film 06 A/Monologue 01) 2013
