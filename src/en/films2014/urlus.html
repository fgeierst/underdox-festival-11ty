Konrad & Kurfürst

 


Esther Urlus – Netherlands 2014 – 7 min.

 

B+K+S+P: Esther Urlus – V: Light Cone

16mm – ohne Dialoge

estherurlus.hotglue.me

 

 

 

 

The home-brewed emulsion is a fragile metaphor for the heroism of Konrad and his horse Kurfurst during the Olympic Games in Berlin, 1936. Falling from his horse, he became a national hero, only to become an anti-hero when overtaken by history. Made by consulting technical publications from early cinema and photographic experiments. – IFF Rotterdam

Tuesday 14/10 10:30 p.m. Werkstattkino

 

Esther Urlus, born in 1966, Netherlands, makes films, performances and installations on 16mm, 35mm and Super-8. Urlus is co-founder and leader of the experimental WORM.filmwerkplaats in Rotterdam.

Films (selection): Abandoned Interiors 2001– Iso 2006 – Idyll 2008 – Audition 2011 – Deep Red 2012 – Rode molen/Red Mill 2013 – Chrome 2013 – Konrad & Kurfurst 2014
