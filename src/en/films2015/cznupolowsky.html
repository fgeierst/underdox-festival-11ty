Okin Cznupolowsky'S LULLABY

 

 

T. Marie – USA 2014

15 min – HD – no dialogues


Sc+P: T. Marie

           


 

 

           


 

"What a homely pastry shop, it perfectly fits for two."

Okin an Vanessa have found a special pastry shop: The Nothosaurus-Café. While reading recent poem-drafts to each other, the music box in the corner plays songs made for humming along and making people grin.

 

tuesday 13 oct 10.30 pm werkstattkino

Okin Cznupolowsky (1985, Munich), currently student at the Academy of Fine Arts Munich.

Films (selection) Chiogga 2004 – Jägerin am Ufer 2013 – Kolyma Beach, second act 2014 – Okin Cznupolowsky’s Playground 2014 (9. UX) – Okin Cznupolowsky's Lullaby 2015
