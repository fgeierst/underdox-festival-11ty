"In her tribute to educational films Marion Pfaus reflects in a very personal way on her own media as well as she achieves the rare combination of avant-garde movie making and slapstick. Through her unique low-fi-art she presents herself in a courageous one-woman-performance that charmingly reflects on the often fatal possibilities of movie making in the era of digital mass media production." (B.B.)

 

Marion Pfaus, born in 1966 lives since 2000 in Berlin. She studied at the Filmacademy Baden-Württemberg in Ludwigsburg. Besides directing short films she performs with interactive media and published her first novel in 2006.
