DESTINATION FINALE

Philip Widmann | GER 2008 | 9 min.

Material: Super-8
Format: MiniDV
Original language: without dialogues

Script: Philip Widmann
Camera: unknown
Sound: Philip Widmann
Editing: Philip Widmann

Production: Gerd Roscher for HfbK Hamburg and Philip Widmann for Works Cited
Print/Sales: Philip Widmann

 

A man travels Europe. Shortly thereafter, American troops enter the ground war in Vietnam. The film is an originial amateur film, shot in 1964, found in Saigon in 2005 and edited by Widmann with a sense of drama.

Philip Widmann lives in Berlin. He graduated in Cultural Anthropology (Hamburg University) and Visual Communications (Academy of Fine Arts Hamburg) and works with film and video. Together with Karsten Krause he founded the audiovisual production cooperative Works Cited in 2008.

Films: Ex Patria 2006 | Destination Finale 2008
